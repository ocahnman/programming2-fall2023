Beef and Broccoli

Description

Beef and Broccoli is a popular and flavorful dish that combines tender strips of beef with vibrant broccoli florets, all coated in a savory, aromatic sauce. It's a staple in Chinese cuisine and has gained popularity worldwide for its delightful taste and simplicity.

In this dish, thinly sliced beef is typically marinated to infuse it with rich flavors, and then it's stir-fried to perfection. The broccoli adds a fresh, crisp element to the dish, making it a well-balanced and healthy choice. The sauce, often made with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and other seasonings, ties everything together with a delicious umami note. Beef and Broccoli is not only a visually appealing dish but also a delightful combination of textures and tastes, making it a go-to option for both home-cooked meals and restaurant dining.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flank steak, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and cornstarch to make the sauce.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add sliced beef and stir-fry until browned. Remove beef from the skillet.
  4. Add broccoli to the skillet and stir-fry until tender.
  5. Return the beef to the skillet, pour in the sauce, and cook until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and broccoli.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Beef and Broccoli!
