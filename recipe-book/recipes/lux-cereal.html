Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Frosted Flakes
  • Orange Caviar
  • Saffron

Cooking Steps

  1. Put Frosted Flakes in a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into the bowl until only the top layer of Frosted Flakes is not submerged.
  3. Top with caviar (don't mix caviar and milk).
  4. Add saffron as garnish.
